Turbo
Swimmming prosthetic for transtibial amputees

"Did you swim before?"
"Yes."
"Do you swim now?"
"No."
"Why?"
"... lots of obstacles."
Exercises do have many benefits, while do exist many barriers for amputees. This is the major project during my master study(02/2019-09/2019). A swimming prosthetic was designed for transtibial amputees. This special prosthetic provides balance and propulsion and helps amputees walk on the bank of swimming pool, change position under water.

This was a individual project, which was completed with the help of my supervisor Luke and my friend Alistair. Always want to say thanks to them.

My work and skills:
1. Planed and conducted the user research with the swimming experts and amputees.
2. Designed mechanism and electronic circuits and programmed by Arduino.
3. Rapid prototyped the functional prototype for testing.
4. Design the apperance and internal structure.
5. Developed the skills in communicating with different people, design thinking, and prototype making andtesting.

The fin rotates following the leg movement.
