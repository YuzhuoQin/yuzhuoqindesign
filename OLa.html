OLa
Children electric toothbrush set

Brushing teeth is of great importance. Teeth health influences human cardiovascular health directly. In this project, I found that children do brush their teeth twice a day as their parents wish, but the problem is how they brush. From my research, no child brushes teeth for more than 2 minutes every time, and they rarely brush their molars. OLa increases brushing time and children's interest by playing music and wobbling body to lead the children to brush all the teeth.

This was a individual project. Thank you very much to my supervisor Jay Yoon.
