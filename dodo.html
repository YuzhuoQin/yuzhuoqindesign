Dodo
baby crib and hanging chair transformation

Baby cribs are necessary for the families that have or is planning to have a baby. But when these babies grow up, baby cribs are not useful anymore and need to be disposed. How can we extend the life of a baby crib? Dodo is a round baby crib which can be use as a hanging chair to be put in your bedroom, living room or garden.
